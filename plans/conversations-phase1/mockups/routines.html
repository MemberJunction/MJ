What: A compact builder that slides in from a chat message you liked — target prefilled from the conversation, a one-glance schedule, and notify settings. Confirms back as an in-chat chip via the reusable ng-user-routines widget.

Why world-class: Captures intent at the exact moment of value ("do this every morning") with near-zero friction — the ChatGPT/Gemini "promote-a-prompt-to-a-schedule" pattern, but native to MJ's conversation surface and zero context-switch.

Tradeoff: Minimal surface trades power for speed — advanced cron, multi-recipient, and monitoring conditions push users over to the full app (A).

What: A time-first surface — an agenda timeline of upcoming scheduled runs (skip / run-now inline) plus a monitoring "Alerts" feed that streams OnChange results, with a calendar view for the week.

Why world-class: Reframes routines as a proactive agentic companion rather than config — you see what your agents are about to do and what they just noticed, which is the emotional payoff of automation and drives ongoing engagement.

Tradeoff: Optimized for at-a-glance awareness, not authoring — creating/editing a routine still hands off to the form in A; risks alert fatigue without good grouping.
