What: A browsable, categorized catalog of skills showing exactly what each bundles (Actions / sub-agents / instructions-only) and its share state. Per-agent toggles plus an AcceptsSkills control (None / Limited / All).

Why world-class: Treats capabilities as a discoverable marketplace — users grasp "what does this give my agent" at a glance, and the AcceptsSkills control makes the governance posture explicit instead of hidden in a settings page.

Tradeoff: Grid scales poorly past a few dozen skills; "enabled where?" is agent-scoped, so the reverse view (which agents use a skill) needs a separate screen.

What: A guided 5-step builder (Name → Instructions → Actions → Sub-agents → Access) framed as teaching the assistant a new capability, with a one-click "capture this workflow as a skill" entry point from a live conversation.

Why world-class: The capture-from-chat hook turns a successful ad-hoc flow into a durable, reusable asset at the moment of insight — zero blank-page friction — and the wizard makes the abstract concept (prompt text + tools) concrete and approachable.

Tradeoff: Wizard is great for creation but heavy for quick edits; the captured-instructions draft can be noisy and needs human curation before it's trustworthy.

Progressive disclosure: catalog shows only name + description until a skill activates — its instructions and tools load on demand, recorded as a Skill run-step.

What: Skills surface as removable chips directly on the agent. During a run, an activation timeline shows precisely when each skill fired and which Actions / sub-agents it unlocked — visualizing the Skill run-step.

Why world-class: Makes invisible prompt-augmentation auditable. The timeline answers "why did the agent do that?" by tying every tool call back to the skill that enabled it, and the progressive-disclosure note explains the load-on-activate cost model.

Tradeoff: Chips are tidy for 2–3 skills but crowd the header at scale; the timeline is reactive (post-hoc) — it shows what activated, not a place to author or govern skills.
