What dues pay for

Your dues fund the work members can't fund alone.

Standards we write

Our published standards are referenced by FDA, EFSA, FSANZ, CFIA, and Codex Alimentarius. Writing and maintaining them takes a paid standards staff plus the volunteer hours of the Standards Committee.

Advocacy we file

Position papers, regulatory comment filings, expert testimony, and the policy researchers and legal counsel who do the work that makes them credible.

Apprenticeships we fund

The MoreCheese Apprenticeship Program places 60 apprentices per year with mentor producers, with a stipend that lets people actually make a living while learning.

Research we publish

The Research Council funds peer-reviewed work at 12 university partners — microbiology, aging-cave science, terroir, sensory science, small-producer economics.

Common questions

FAQ

Can I switch tiers mid-year?

Yes — switch any time from your member portal. Difference in dues is pro-rated. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal anniversary.

Do students get a discount?

Full-time students at an Institutional-member program join at the Apprentice tier for $45 (52% off). Email students@morecheese.org with proof of enrollment.

Is there a hardship discount?

Yes. We never want dues to be a barrier. Email membership@morecheese.org — no documentation required, no questions asked.

What if I'm not a cheesemaker?

Many of our members aren't — we include affineurs, mongers, retailers, distributors, scientists, educators, journalists, and serious enthusiasts. The craft is bigger than the kitchen.

Can my organization sponsor instead?

Corporate sponsorship is a separate program — see About → Sponsors for the tier structure and what's included. Sponsors are visible, not members.
