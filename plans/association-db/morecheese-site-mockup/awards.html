National Cheese Awards · 2026

The most respected recognition in the craft.

Five competitions per year. 29 certified judges. 1,100+ entries from 38 countries. Blind tasting in 12 categories. Peer-reviewed. Apolitical. Held at the close of every Annual Conference.

How the awards work

Blind tasting · Peer-reviewed · Calibrated

Entry

Producer-tier members get 4 free entries per year. Each entry includes a 1kg sample shipped to the judging facility plus a 1-page producer narrative (used only for context after scoring is final).

Blind tasting

Each entry is coded and presented to a 3-judge panel without producer information. Judges score on a 100-point rubric covering paste, rind, aroma, flavor, balance, finish, and category fidelity.

Awards

Gold, Silver, Bronze awarded in each category by absolute score (not ranking). Best in Show selected from category Golds by a 9-judge super-panel. Results announced at the Awards Gala.
