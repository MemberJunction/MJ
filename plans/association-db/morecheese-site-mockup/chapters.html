Chapters

Fifteen chapters · six continents · one federation.

The federation runs regionally because cheese is regional. Each chapter has its own elected chair, quarterly programming, regional advocacy focus, and a chapter operations budget funded by chapter-share of member dues.

Form a new chapter

Don't see your region?

A new chapter requires 25 founding members in a contiguous region, a proposed chair, and a charter draft. The federation provides templating, programming guidance, and a one-year operations subsidy. Talk to us.
