House rules

A code of conduct that gets enforced.

We have a Community Code of Conduct and it isn't decorative. Moderation is handled by elected member moderators, one per category. We've banned 14 accounts in our 4-year forum history — most for proselytizing about non-cheese topics, a couple for unkindness toward apprentices. Disagreement about cheese is encouraged. Bad behavior isn't.
