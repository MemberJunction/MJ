Standards Library

Industry-wide standards · written by the people who make cheese.

79 years of published standards. Referenced by FDA, EFSA, FSANZ, CFIA, and Codex Alimentarius. Maintained by the Standards Committee and the nine Standards Working Groups. Adopted via member vote at the Annual Plenary.

How standards get made

From working-group draft to ratified standard.

01
WG drafts
A Standards Working Group authors a draft over multiple sessions. Public WG members + Standards Committee + outside experts.
02
Committee review
Standards Committee reviews the draft, may request revisions, votes to advance to the Annual Plenary.
03
Plenary vote
All Professional / Producer / Lifetime members vote during the Annual Plenary. Simple majority required.
04
Publish
Adopted standards are published with an effective date, deprecation of any superseded version, and submitted to FDA / EFSA / Codex liaisons.

Aging-Cave Conditions & Documentation

Temperature, humidity, air-exchange, sanitation, and documentation requirements for affinage facilities. Applies to traditional caves and engineered controlled-environment facilities.

ICF-STD-015 · Rev 1 · 2022Published

Sensory Evaluation Rubric (100-point)

The 100-point rubric used in National Cheese Awards judging and in member sensory training. Categories: paste, rind, aroma, flavor, balance, finish, category fidelity. Calibration protocol for judging panels.
