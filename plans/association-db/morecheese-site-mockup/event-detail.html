Overview

A federation gathered.

The Annual Conference is the only time the federation gathers in one place. Members come for the Standards Plenary (the only forum where formal votes are taken), the National Cheese Awards gala (110 finalists this year, 29 judges, 12 categories), the Producer-Only Forum (closed-door, members-only business sessions), and the four parallel workshop tracks running across all four days.

If this is your first ICF Annual, plan to attend the Newcomer Reception on Wednesday night — chapter chairs and committee leadership host an informal welcome over a flight of award nominees.

Four parallel tracks

Production & Technique

Hands-on workshops on washed rinds, bloomy rinds, alpines, hard cheeses, fresh cheeses. Bring an apron.

Science & Research

Microbiology, aging-cave engineering, terroir science, sensory methodology, food-safety research.

Business & Operations

Scaling, distribution, FDA tier transitions, retail, financing, succession planning.

Standards & Policy

The Plenary itself, plus policy workshops on labeling, advocacy, regulation, and international harmonization.
