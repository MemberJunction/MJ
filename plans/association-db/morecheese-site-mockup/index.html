Membership

A federation built by its members.

From the apprentice making their first wheel to the third-generation producer running a 12-person creamery — members shape every standard, every position, every program.

Five membership tiers, scaled to your stage. The benefits compound: certification discounts, voting rights in the standards plenary, eligibility to chair committees, free continuing education, and access to the member-only research library.

Advocacy

Where the federation stands.

All positions

Active · United States

FDA Dairy Labeling Proposal — Support with Revisions

Position adopted June 12, 2026. We support clearer terroir-of-origin labeling but push back on the proposed pasteurization disclosure language for traditional cheeses.

Standards Committee · 12 advocacy actions Read position

Active · European Union

EU Raw-Milk Cheese Carve-Out — Support

We support the Italian-French joint proposal to formally protect raw-milk traditional cheeses from the proposed pasteurization mandate. Position paper co-authored with Slow Food.

European Chapter · 23 actions Read position

Active · Federal/State

Small-Producer Inspection Modernization Act — Endorse

We endorse the bipartisan US bill modernizing FDA inspection protocols for sub-100k-lb dairies. Letter signed by 412 member producers.

Policy Working Group · 47 actions Read position
