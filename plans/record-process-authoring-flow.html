Bulk Processes & Field Rules

A business user defines reusable rules to update any entity's records — fixed values, other fields, formulas, lookups, or an AI prompt — then runs them on demand against a view, a list, a filter, or just the rows they've selected. Always with a safe dry-run preview first.
