Cross-cutting standards

Beyond the dedicated guides, the CLAUDE.md in the repo root is the canonical developer handbook covering naming conventions, anti-patterns (any, weak singletons, dynamic import()), Angular best practices, RunView optimization, the design token system, and more. We recommend reading it cover-to-cover at least once.

If a section of CLAUDE.md outgrows it, that's a cue to promote it into /guides/.
