Cheese Puns & Fun Facts Corner 🧀
"You're the Big Cheese!"
This idiom dates back to early 20th century America, when large wheels of cheese were status symbols. Around the world, cheese appears in countless expressions of excellence and importance!
The Science of Grate-ness
Why does cheese make everything better? It's all about umami! Aged cheeses contain glutamates that trigger our fifth taste receptor, creating an explosion of savory satisfaction that's literally irresistible to our brains.
World Record Wheel
The world's largest cheese wheel weighed over 57,000 pounds! Created in Wisconsin in 1988, this massive cheddar took 170,000 pounds of milk to make. Talk about thinking big!
