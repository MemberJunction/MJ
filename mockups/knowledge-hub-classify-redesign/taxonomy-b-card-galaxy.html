Design notes — Option B

Card-based "galaxy" view: each card is a tag, badges encode governance state, stats footer encodes health. Drill into a card to enter its subtree (breadcrumb up top to navigate back). Trade depth for visual richness — you only ever see one level at a time, but each tag's status is immediate and beautiful.

  • Strengths: visually engaging; great for browsing and exploration; governance state is immediately legible; scope tinting (purple wash) communicates "this is restricted" without reading.
  • Weaknesses: editing requires opening a modal/drawer; comparing siblings across the tree is hard; expensive on screen real estate.
  • Best for: stakeholders browsing the taxonomy ("what tags do we have?"); quarterly governance audits.
  • Distinctive: color-tinted cards by state (purple = scoped, blue = frozen, orange = alert); per-card health stats footer; "enter" affordance on parents.
