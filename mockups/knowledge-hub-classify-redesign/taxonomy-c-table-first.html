Design notes — Option C

Spreadsheet-mode for taxonomy. Built around governance audits at scale: "show me every tag where AutoGrow is on AND parent is frozen", "set MinWeight to 0.85 across all of Compliance". Inline-editable cells, multi-select, indentation visible without losing tabular structure.

  • Strengths: highest density of governance information; bulk operations are first-class; sort by Pending or AvgConf surfaces problem tags fast; CSV export native.
  • Weaknesses: intimidating; descriptions / synonyms hidden behind row-click; loses the visual "tree-ness" that B and A preserve.
  • Best for: data-governance roles; quarterly audits; large taxonomies (1,000+ tags).
  • Distinctive: indentation-as-row-padding to keep the tree shape; mini-toggle pills for boolean cells; inline-editable numeric cells; alert-row tinting for wide-node.
