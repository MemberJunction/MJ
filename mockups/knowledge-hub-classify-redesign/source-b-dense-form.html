Design notes — Option B

Power-user surface. Everything on one page, grouped into six numbered sections. Sticky right-rail navigation jumps between groups; preview card shows the diff at a glance. Threshold sliders sit on a threshold-band visualization that mirrors the Suggestions Inbox detail view, reinforcing the same metaphor across the app.

  • Strengths: fastest for editing; everything visible without state-juggling; great for SE/admins; preview card shows diff impact.
  • Weaknesses: overwhelming first time; can't enforce "set thresholds before budgets"; long page on small screens.
  • Best for: ops folks tweaking dozens of sources.
  • Distinctive: threshold-band slider pair; segmented mode picker (compact vs. wizard's big cards); sticky preview-of-changes panel.
