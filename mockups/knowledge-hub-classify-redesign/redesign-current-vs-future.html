Source Configuration

Taxonomy mode, thresholds, tag root, budgets — the knobs that define a classifier — aren't in the dashboard. They live on the Content Sources entity form, reached by "Open advanced settings →" which navigates you out of the app.

Add two top-level tabs (Inbox, Health) and enrich the tag editor with Governance · Synonyms · Scope panels. Lowest adoption cost — keeps the mental model people already have, drops new surfaces beside the old ones.
